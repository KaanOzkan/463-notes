Workflow models

Workflow is a series of tasks to accomplish a business use case

Scenario is one full execution path through a use case, listing observable actions

Alternative use case: a sub use case that achieved the goal through different "intended" actions (Cached response)

Exception: sub use case that captures an unwanted but possible deviation

Activity Diagram: UML model expressing the flow of control

Dataflow Diagram (DFD) functional decomposition of the work, and the data dependencies between functions
